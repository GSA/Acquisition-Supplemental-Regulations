PGI 226.1—INDIAN INCENTIVE PROGRAM


PGI 226.103 Procedures.


(1) Submit a request for funding of the Indian incentive to the Office of Small Business Programs, Office of the Under Secretary of Defense (Acquisition, Technology, and Logistics) (OUSD(AT&L) SBP), 201 12th Street South, Suite 406, Arlington, VA 22202.


(2) Upon receipt of funding from OUSD(AT&L) SBP, issue a contract modification to add the Indian incentive funding for payment of the contractor s request for adjustment as described in the clause at DFARS 252.226-7001, Utilization of Indian Organizations, Indian-Owned Economic Enterprises, and Native Hawaiian Small Business Concerns.
