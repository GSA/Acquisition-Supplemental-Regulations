AF PGI 5336

Construction and Architect-Engineer Contracts

AF PGI 5336.9001 Forms

Contracting officers are encouraged to use existing contractor-developed forms to the maximum extent practicable. In those instances where commercial forms are not available, the following templates may be used to administer construction programs:

  1. Contract Inspection and Acceptance Report

  2. Construction Cost Estimate Breakdown

  3. Schedule of Material Submittals

  4. Contract Bonds Checklist

  5. Pre-performance Conference Checklist

AF PGI 5336.9002 Air Force Construction Guide

Contracting officers and all acquisition team personnel involved with SABER contracts should refer to the Air Force Contracting Construction Guide to ensure compliance with regulations and best practices, both prior to and during the acquisition planning phase.
