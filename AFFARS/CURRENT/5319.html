SUBPART 5319.8 — CONTRACTING WITH THE SMALL BUSINESS ADMINISTRATION [THE 8(A) PROGRAM]

5319.810 SBA Appeals

When notified by the SBA that it has filed an appeal with the Agency Head, the contracting officer should notify the local Small Business Office and follow MP5319 to prepare an appeal file. Forward the appeal file through the SCO to SAF/SB to arrive in SAF/SB within ten workdays after receipt of the formal appeal with a courtesy copy to the MAJCOM Small Business Office.
