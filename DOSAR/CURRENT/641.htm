PART 641 – ACQUISITION OF UTILITY SERVICES

Subpart 641.2 – Acquiring Utility Services

641.201 Policy.

(b) If a contracting officer at an overseas post determines that it is not feasible to obtain utility services by purchase order, blanket purchase agreement, or contract, the contracting officer may pay the utility bill based on evidence of services provided. Such actions must be coordinated with the post Financial Management Officer. “Utility services”, as defined by FAR 41.101, means a service such as furnishing electricity, natural or manufactured gas, water, sewerage, thermal energy, chilled water, steam, hot water, or high temperature hot water. It does not include telephone or other telecommunication services.

(d) The Procurement Executive is the agency head for the purposes of FAR 41.201(d)(2)(i) and FAR 41.201(d)(3).
