652.228-74, Defense Base Act Insurance Rates – Limitation – Fixed-Price

652.242-71, Notice of Shipment

652.242-73, Authorization and Performance (Alternate I for local guard contracts only)

652.243-70, Notices

Note 2:

The following provisions/clauses are prescribed for use only for contracts to be awarded and/or performed in the United States, it territories, and possessions:

652.219-70, Department of State Subcontracting Goals

Note 3:

The following clause is prescribed for use in SBA 8(a) contracts issued under the Memorandum of Understanding:

652.219-71, Section 8(a) Direct Awards

Note 4:

The following clause is prescribed for use in personal services contracts awarded in support of INL programs overseas:

652.237-70, Compensatory Time Off

Note 5:

The following provision is prescribed for use only for overseas local guard services solicitations.

652.237-73 Statement of Qualifications for Preference as a U.S. Person
