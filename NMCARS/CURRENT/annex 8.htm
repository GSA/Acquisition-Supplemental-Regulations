ANNEX 8 - PEER REVIEW PROGRAM

Reporting requirements. DON Contracting Activities shall submit by the 25th day of every month, a rolling forecast of acquisitions requiring peer reviews by DASN(P) or DPC. Use the DON Peer Review Projections spreadsheet format when submitting reports. Submit reports via email to RDAJ&As.fct@navy.mil with the subject: “DFARS 201.170 – Quarterly Peer Review Projections.” The spreadsheet template is located on the ASN(RDA) Policy website http://www.secnav.navy.mil/rda/Pages/NMCARS.aspx.
