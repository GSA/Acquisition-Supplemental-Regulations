ANNEX 8 - PEER REVIEW PROGRAM

Reporting requirements.DON Contracting Activities shall submit by the 25th day of every month, a rolling forecast of acquisitions requiring peer reviews by DASN(AP) or DPC which provides the following information:

Command/Buying Activity (include Location)

•Peer Review Authority (DPC, DASN(AP))

•Program or contract action (Description)

•Supply or Service

•Contract Type

•Estimated value (Inclusive of options)

•Competition (Yes or No)

•Anticipated Review Dates for the following:

•Pre-Solicitation Review

•Pre-BCM/PNM or Pre-BAFO Review

•Post-BCM/PNM or Pre-Award Review

•Required Award Date or Option Exercise Date

•Buying Activity POC (Name/telephone number/email address)

•Identify ACAT/BCAT or MAIS level, if applicable

Use the DON Peer Review Projections spreadsheet format when submitting reports. Submit via email to http://www.abilityone.gov/procurement_list/index.html with the subject: DFARS 201.170 Quarterly Peer Review Projections.
