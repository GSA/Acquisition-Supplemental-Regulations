5225.7009-4 Waiver.

Submit the proposed waiver via DASN(P) by email at https://psctool.us with the subject “[Activity Name] DFARS 225.7009-4 - Ball and Roller Bearings Restriction Waiver.” The waiver will be in the form of a written D&F containing a certification that:

(a) Adequate domestic supplies are not available to meet DOD requirements on a timely basis; and

(b) The acquisition must be made in order to acquire capability for national security purposes.

Parent topic: 5225.7009 Restriction on ball and roller bearings.
