ANNEX 8 - PEER REVIEW ROLLING FORECAST

Reporting requirements. DON Contracting Activities shall submit by the 25th day of every

month, a rolling forecast of acquisitions requiring peer reviews by DASN(P) or DPC. Use the DON Peer Review Projections spreadsheet format when submitting reports. Submit reports by email to https://psctool.us with the subject: “DFARS 201.170 – Quarterly Peer Review Projections.” The spreadsheet template is located on the ASN(RDA) Policy website

https://psctool.us.
