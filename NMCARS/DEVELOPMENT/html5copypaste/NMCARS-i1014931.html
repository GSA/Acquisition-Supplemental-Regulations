5205.404 Release of longrange acquisition forecasts.

Submit an annual long-range acquisition forecast, using the format provided in Annex 25, to

DASN(P) by email with the subject “[Activity Name] FAR 5.404 Long Range Acquisition

Forecast” by 20 June annually.
