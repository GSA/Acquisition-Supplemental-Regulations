5234.201 Policy.

(1)(i) DASN(P) Class Deviation 15-C-913 dated October 7, 2015 waives the application of Earned Value Management (EVM) in Level of Effort (LOE) cost or incentive type contracts valued at $20,000,000 or greater; the application of EVM for cost or incentive type LOE CLINs valued at $20 million or more in mixed contracts; and EVM provisions prescribed in DFARS Subpart 234. Mixed contracts are defined as a contract containing FFP CLINs as well as cost CLINs, incentive CLINs or both cost and incentive CLINs. In these situations, program managers and contracting officers shall utilize alternative methods to monitor contractor cost and performance.
