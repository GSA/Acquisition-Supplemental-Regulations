5222.1006 Solicitation provisions and contract clauses.

(c)(1) The https://psctool.us contains DON guidance on processing price adjustments under FAR 52.222-43 and FAR 52.222-44 that result from:

(i) Changes in wage determinations issued under the Service Contract Labor Standards statute, or

(ii) Amendments changing the minimum wage required by the Fair Labor Standards Act.

(S-90) The DON’s Price Adjustment Calculation Tool (PACT) is available to automate the calculation of Service Contract Labor Standards price adjustments and streamline the process for contractors and contracting officers. PACT is available at https://psctool.us.
