5201.603-2-90 Contracting officer warrants.

Notification requirements. Submit copies of documentation supporting any warrants authorized pursuant to DFARS 201.603-2(3) and warrants issued pursuant to the exception at DFARS 201.603- 2(2)(ii) to DASN(P) at https://psctool.us with the subject “DFARS 201.603-2(2)(ii) – Waiver of Contracting Officer Qualification” within five days of issuance.

Parent topic: 5201.603 Selection, appointment, and termination of appointment for contracting officers.
