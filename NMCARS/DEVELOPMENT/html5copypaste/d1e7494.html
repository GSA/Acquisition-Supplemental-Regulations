ANNEX 8 - PEER REVIEW ROLLING FORECAST

Reporting requirements. DON Contracting Activities shall submit quarterly, by March 10, June 10, September 10, and December 10, a rolling forecast of acquisitions requiring peer reviews by

DASN(P) or DPC. Use the DON Peer Review Projections spreadsheet format when submitting reports. Submit reports by email to RDAJ&As.fct@navy.mil with the subject:

“DFARS 201.170 – Quarterly Peer Review Projections.” The electronic version of this template can be found at https://www.secnav.navy.mil/rda/DASN-P/Pages/NMCARS.aspx.
