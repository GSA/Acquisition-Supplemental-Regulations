5212.7102-2 Reporting requirements.

(a) The annual report identifying all contracts awarded under the Pilot Program on Acquisition of Military Purpose Nondevelopmental Items during the preceding fiscal year shall be submitted to DASN(P) by October 15 of each year by email at https://psctool.us with the subject “[Activity Name] DFARS 212.7102-3(a) - Pilot Program For Acquisition Of Military-Purpose Nondevelopmental Items.”

Parent topic: 5212.7102 Pilot program.
