5206.303-92 Bridge Contract Reporting.

Data on bridge contract use shall be reported by HCAs to DASN(P). Reports shall be submitted within 30 days of the end of each quarter in the format prescribed by Annex 5. The required Excel file for reporting can be found at the ASN(RDA) website. Negative reports are required. Reports shall be submitted by email at https://psctool.us with the subject “NMCARS 5206.303-92 - Bridge Contract Report by [Command Name]”.
