5225.770-5 Waiver of prohibition.

(a) Waivers pursuant to DFARS 225.770-5 shall be submitted via DASN(P) by email at https://psctool.us with the subject “[Activity Name] DFARS 225.770-5 Waiver - Certain Items from Communist Chinese Military Companies.”

(c)(1) A copy of waivers submitted to DPC pursuant to DFARS 225.770-5(a) shall be submitted to DASN(P) by email at https://psctool.us with the subject “[Activity Name] DFARS 225.770-5(a) Waiver—Certain Items from Communist Chinese Military Companies.”

Parent topic: 5225.770 Prohibition on acquisition of certain items from Communist Chinese military companies.
