PART 2817
SPECIAL CONTRACTING METHODS  

    SUBPART 2817.1 - MULTIYEAR CONTRACTING

2817.108 Congressional notification.

    Pursuant to FAR 17.108(a), the original congressional notification shall be submitted to PPRG, JMD, for concurrence, coordination to the Attorney General, and subsequent transmission to the appropriate congressional committees.

    SUBPART 2817.6 - MANAGEMENT AND OPERATING CONTRACTS

2817.605 Award, renewal, and extension.

    In accordance with FAR 17.605(b), the contracting officer, following bureau procedures, shall review each management and operation contract, at appropriate intervals and at least once every 5 years.
