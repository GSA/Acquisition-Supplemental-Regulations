TRANSFARS PART 5503
IMPROPER BUSINESS PRACTICES AND PERSONAL CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

SUBPART 5503.1 – SAFEGUARDS


5503.104-7 Violations or Possible Violations.
(a)

(1) The contracting officer must forward the information and determination required by FAR 3.104-7(a)(1) to the clearance approval authority of the affected procurement and to TCJA. Where the HCA is the clearance approval authority, the Division Chief shall serve as the individual designated for review.
