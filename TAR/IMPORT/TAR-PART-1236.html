Subpart 1236.5 - Contract Clauses

1236.570 Special precautions for work at operating airports.

Where any acquisition will require work at an operating airport, insert the clause at (TAR) 48 CFR 1252.236-70, Special Precautions for Work at Operating Airports, in solicitations and contracts.
