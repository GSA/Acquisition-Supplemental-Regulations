Subpart 1214.3 - Submission of Bids

1214.302 Bid submission.

(b)

(1) Contracting officers may permit telegraphic bids to be communicated by means of a telephone call from the telegraph office to the designated office provided that procedures and controls have been established by the COCO for receiving and safeguarding these incoming bids.
