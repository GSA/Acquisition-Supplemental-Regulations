PART 1211 - DESCRIBING AGENCY NEEDS

SUBPART 1211.1--SELECTING AND DEVELOPING REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENTS

1211.101 Order of precedence for requirements documents.

Safeguards to ensure safety, security (including sensitive information and information technology security) and environmental protection shall be included, as applicable, in requirements documents.

SUBPART 1211.2--USING AND MAINTAINING REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENTS

1211.204-70 Solicitation provisions and contract clauses.

The contracting officer shall insert the clause at (TAR) 48 CFR 1252.211-70, Index for Specifications, when an index or table of contents may be furnished with the specification.
