12.403 Termination.

(b) Policy. Contracting officers shall coordinate contract/order termination actions with the CoCO, HCO and legal counsel and, if the contractor is a small business concern, with the Office of Small Business Programs (OSBP). The HCO and HCA and will be updated in weekly DITCO SITREPs.
