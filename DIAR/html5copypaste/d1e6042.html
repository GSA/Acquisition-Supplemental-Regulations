Subpart 1451.1 - Contractor Use of Government Supply Sources

1451.102 Authorization to use Government supply sources.

If the CO decides to authorize a contractor to use Government supply sources under the conditions prescribed in FAR 51.102, a written request for a FEDSTRIP activity address code (See FPMR 101-26.203) shall be made through the acquisition office FEDSTRIP point of contact.
